Ay [Third Raikage]

"One finger is enough."

Cost: T300

Archetype: Rushdown/Snowball

Difficulty: [*****]

Designer Notes

Decided to go base only as he doesn't have a lot, but the quality of moves would be fun for a kit.
My vision with him is a rushdown with some pressuring involved, though not a perfect pressurer. I'd like for him to be a perfect rushdown although but lemme know what you think.
Ofc due to no awakening his values are enhanced more than the usual base to offset that loss.

Base Moveset

[C] Nukite

Nukite dictates Ay's current power draw and mirrors the awakening meter with five visible bars.

Changing is done by using Utility, in which a cutscene shows Ay changing to the finger tier. This has no I-Frames but it gives Hyper Armor.

Five Finger provides Impale.
Four Finger provides Cross.
Three Finger provides Lightning Cloak while replacing Impale with Hell Thrust and Cross with Hell Wave.
One Finger provides Hell Stab.

Notes

Properties: Buff, Cutscene, Hyper Armor

Passive

M1s increase by 0.5 for each stack filled. To the maximum of 5 stacks that would be + 2.5 M1 damage.

[1] Lightning Cloak

Ay channels chakra into a cloak of lightning while a bolt crashes down on his position to punish nearby enemies. It grants faster movement and teleport dashes for 20 seconds.

If activated during Endure this becomes Kuroi, where Ay surges black lightning through his body, sending it outwards in a violent burst. If Lightning Cloak is already active, the cloak is consumed for a bit more damage.

Notes

Properties: AOE, Buff, Super Armor

This move is supposed to be near instantly applied. Though since it has Super Armor it could be cancelled (somehow)
As you can tell, Kuroi is not canon in any media, I made it from creative liberty considering Ay was the inventor of black lightning chakra. So just for funsies!

[2] Impale

Ay teleports forward, stopping before an opponent, and impales them.
In combo, launches the opponent.

[2] Hell Thrust

Ay holds his hand out to the side and starts freely sprinting. On contact with an opponent, Ay lunges past them, damaging and crumpling them.
Can't be used mid-air, or in combo.

Notes

Properties: Combo Starter, Knockback, Wall Bounce

His main tool for getting close. It would have a medium cd, not too low or too high

Properties: Free Movement Grab, Stagger, Combo Starter, Block Bypass

Would have high damage
Attack's a bit op but I believe it's fine with the start up and the distance needed covered

[3] Cross

Ay holds his hand diagonally, then cuts across to damage the opponent.

[3] Hell Wave

Ay slings his hand across to send forward a giant blast of air, ragdolling opponents. Can be used after M1 finishers.

If held, Ay focuses his chakra to send forward a slice of air. It travels while launching enemies for higher damage and can break block.

Notes

Properties: Combo Extender

Simple bnb. Obviously very low cd due to not a lot going on

Properties: Ragdoll/Launcher, Block Breaker

[4] Endure

Ay braces himself to tank incoming attacks. Damage will not be reduced, but he can't get grabbed or interrupted.
Towards the end, movement can be inputted before the effects end.
If hit at least twice before the state of said walking, Ay will immediately gain one stack and skip to the next tier of Nukite.
If Lightning Cloak is used during this, the combination becomes Kuroi.

Notes

Properties: Ultra Armor, Buff

Better not hit him!
Decided to make it two hits as its more balanced than one. Considering 3 hits but idk, 2 seems to be fine

[G] Hell Stab

Ay stomps the ground, spiking the ground forward and launching the opponent up on hit. He dashes forward with his One Finger Nukite and catches the opponent as they fall down, going past them. The amount of chakra and intensity of it creates an outburst of lightning chakra to damage the opponent and anyone nearby.

Notes

Properties: Ultimate, Block Bypass, Ranged Grab, AOE, Hyper Armor

Replacing awakening. This cuts straight to ultimate, around 50 dmg I'd say.
Couldn't decide between Super or Hyper Armor, but I think Hyper is fine, Super would be too weak
